Approximately 1.4 million children around the globe suffer from blindness and almost triple suffer from some form of visual impairment. Therefore, it is vital that we do everything in our ability to help these children feel more comfortable with themselves and accepted in society. Our activities and interactive video games are specially designed to stimulate the brain and sharpen the dominant senses while our young friends enjoy themselves. Our main goal as a team is to create a safe space for children, where they can feel comfortable, loved and confident in their abilities. By interacting with one-another through online games and activities, they will get the chance to engage in new friendships and build up their self-esteem. We welcome you to ViCommunity!

Teachers Teachers

The topic of child blindness and visual impairment inside a classroom has been a taboo topic for a very long time. There are very few facilities that are suitable for children with such conditions. In addition, there is a lack of teaching staff, specially trained to teach visually impaired children. Therefore, as an educator, it is your duty to step up and be here for all the children that need you. Specially designed programs and class activities are provided on our website to help make the lesson more fun. You have the chance to interact with parents in forums or question-and-answer discussion fields. We welcome you to ViCommunity!
